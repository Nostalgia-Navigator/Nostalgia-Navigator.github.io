Project Timeless

Introduction

Take the seat of an old, mustard-colored J35A Draken fighter jet and stop the nefarious, time-traveling Baron von Schtopwatch in this top-down shoot-em-up! Smash apart massive squadrons of historically-themed enemy planes, bomb the pesky ground units, and blow up the boss plane to advance to the next level! Just don't get blown up by the barrage of wacky bullets, and don't shoot the goodies!

Project Timeless is a fan remake of Wingnuts: Temporal Navigator with 3D graphics. It is made in Godot Engine and 100% free.

Background

Wingnuts: Temporal Navigator was a 2006 Mac OS X game by Freeverse, Inc. It is like Time Pilot with the addition of higher difficulty, sprawling levels, and hectic combat. While Freeverse, Inc may have since gone under, surviving copies of the game still exist.

Contribute

Project Timeless aims to recreate Wingnuts: Temporal Navigator as faithfully as possible. We would appreciate any useful data on the game internals and content, as well as 3D recreations of the original assets.
